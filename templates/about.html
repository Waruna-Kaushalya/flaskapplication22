About VEGEPredictor

This VEGEPredictor web application was implemented for final year individual research of BSc (Hons) Computing - National Institute of Business Management degree program. This web application identifies past data patterns and predicts the extend of land to be cultivated and the harvest accordingly. This web application's main objective is to educate farmers through farmer regulators to reduce the amount of excess vegetables by cultivating according to a pattern.
